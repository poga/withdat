A collection of use cases with Dat and its underlying technology

Dat is a grant-funded, open-source, decentralized data sharing tool for efficiently versioning and syncing changes to Data.

We believe that Dat and its core technology, hypercore and hyperdrive, can be the infrastructure of a decentralized web.

Here's a list of projects/prototypes/experiments about the possibility of dat and a decentralized future.
